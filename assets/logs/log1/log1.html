The Journey Starts Here!

Posted on August 18, 2023
Game Design

I've always been a fan of tabletop card games and board games and creating my own has always interested me. These logs will follow my attempt at creating one and hopefully lead to a successful game one day. The idea here isn't to make millions straight away but to learn as much as I can about the whole process.

Overtime I would write my ideas down on my phone, building up a collection of themes and concepts. What I found at this stage was if I didn't stop and attempt to create a prototype then I'll be at this stage forever. The truth was I wouldn't know if I was happy with the concept until I tested it.

The Beginning Of An Idea

I picked what I think is my simplest idea and I decided to create an initial rules document, detailing the minimum requirements. I tried not to get too carried away with all these weird and wonderful rules and mechanics. For now the simpler the better. What I landed on was a simple potion recipe making game, the general concept was some cards represent recipes and some ingredients. Players would have to collect the required ingredients to complete a number of recipes before the other players. You can find my initial rules draft here. The theme I decided to start with was a scaly theme, I opted for potion names and ingredients which would be suited to a world ruled by lizards.

Now I had my draft of rules and a rough theme, I decided to list out some names, types and effects for potential cards. I have about 50 recipes, 50 ingredients and 50 actions. I narrowed this down for a final draft as I'd be simulating a 2 player game. You can find the cards that I settled on here.
